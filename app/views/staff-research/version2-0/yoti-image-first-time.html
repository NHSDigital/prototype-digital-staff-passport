Scan the QR code to share your digital identity with your organisation

Once you have Yoti or Post Office EasyID set up, you can scan the QR code to share your verified digital identity with your organisation.

What to do if you cannot create a digital identity

You can still register even if:

  • the digital identity check does not work for your passport or biometric card
  • you change your mind about creating a digital identity

To register, select 'back' and choose a face-to-face identity check with a member of the organisation's HR team.
