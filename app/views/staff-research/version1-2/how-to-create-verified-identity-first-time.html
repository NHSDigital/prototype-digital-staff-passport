How to create a verified digital identity

Download Yoti or Post Office EasyID

You can create your digital identity with either Yoti or Post Office EasyID. These are government-approved smartphone apps for verifying identities. Your digital identity can replace an in-person ID check.

Your verified digital identity can replace the need for an in-person ID check. You can still attend an in-person check with your organisation if you would prefer to do this instead.

Get Yoti from:

  • the App Store
  • the Google Play Store

Get Post Office EasyID from:

  • the App Store
  • the Google Play Store

Most people can finish applying for a digital identity in about 15 minutes. Please complete it in one go.

You'll need to use your smartphone camera to scan your face and your passport on Yoti or Post Office EasyID.

Share your digital identity with your organisation

You'll need to use your smartphone camera to scan your face and your passport on Yoti or Post Office EasyID.
