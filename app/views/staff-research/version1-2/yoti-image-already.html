Scan the QR code to share your digital identity with your organisation

If you already have Yoti or Post Office EasyID set up, you can scan the QR code to share your verified digital identity with your organisation.

What to do if you can't create a digital identity

The digital identity check may not work for some passports or biometric cards. If you change your mind about creating a digital identity you can still register for a Digital Staff Passport.

In both cases, you'll need to select 'back' and choose a face-to-face identity check with a member of the organisation's HR team.
