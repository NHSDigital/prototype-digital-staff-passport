Digital Staff Passport

NHS employees can use Digital Staff Passport to request, share and review essential information, like employment history, core skills and occupational health. Joining the Digital Staff Passport can also make onboarding easier, faster and less repetitive.

You are signing up for a Digital Staff Passport with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals. You can change the organisation if this isn't the right one.

Before you start

You will need a smartphone and your passport.

We'll ask you to download:

  • either the Yoti or Post Office EasyID app
  • have your passport ready to scan
  • Microsoft Authenticator to your smartphone
