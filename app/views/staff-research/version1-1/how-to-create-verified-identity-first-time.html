How to create a verified digital identity

You can create your digital identity with either Yoti or Post Office EasyID. These are government-approved smartphone apps for verifying identities. Your digital identity can replace an in-person ID check.

Yoti and Post Office EasyID are available for both Apple and Android devices. Learn more about installing and using a digital identity app.

Registering for a digital identity can take up to 20 minutes. It will involve uploading a scan of your:

  • face, using the front camera of your smartphone
  • passport

Once you have registered, select 'continue' to share your ID with your organisation.

What to do if the verified digital identity does not work

The digital identity check may not work for some passports or biometric cards. You'll need to attend an in-person identity check with a member of the organisation's HR team.

If you change your mind, you can still register for Digital Staff Passport without a digital identity. You'll need to attend an in-person identity check.
