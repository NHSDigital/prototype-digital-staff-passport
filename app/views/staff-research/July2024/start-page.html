Digital Staff Passport

Sign up to Digital Staff Passport to make onboarding easier, faster and less repetitive for NHS employees moving between trusts.

You can use this service to request, share and review essential employment information.

Before you start

We'll ask you to:

  • have your smartphone ready and check it has signal
  • set up your digital identity using either Yoti or EasyID on your smartphone
  • have your passport ready to scan
  • download Microsoft Authenticator
  • check that your employment details are correct

This application can take up to 30 minutes to complete. You will not be able to save and come back to your application.
