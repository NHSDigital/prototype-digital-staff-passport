Digital Staff Passport

Digital Staff Passport makes onboarding easier, faster and less repetitive for NHS employees moving between trusts.

You can use this service:

  • if you are a postgraduate doctor in training or a temporary mover
  • to share essential employment information including pre-employment checks, core skills and occupational health
  • to request, share, review credentials

Before you start

We'll ask you to:

  • have your smartphone ready and check it has signal
  • set up your digital identity using either Yoti or Post Office ID, the government-approved smartphone apps for verifying identities
  • have your passport ready to scan when prompted by the identity verification app
  • download Microsoft Authenticator to store your credentials and access key
  • check that the details about your employment type and status are correct on the employee portal
  • a passport photo
