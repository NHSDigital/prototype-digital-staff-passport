Start your onboarding

Complete the following steps to start onboarding with your new organisation.

Verify your identity with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals

You can do this by:

  • sharing your digital identity credential which is in your Microsoft Authenticator app
  • attending a face-to-face identity check with the HR team at your new organisation

Check your profile

Your existing DSP already stores your personal details, pre-employment checks and other important information.

Log into your profile to check and share essential employment information with your new organisation.

Once your identity is confirmed by your new trust, you'll receive an access key. Add this access key to your Microsoft Authenticator app. Use your access key to login to your new organisation and collect your digital identity credential.

Your registration with your new organisation will then be complete. You'll be able to use your Digital Staff Passport to receive and share credentials with Sheffield Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust.
