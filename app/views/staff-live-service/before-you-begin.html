Before you begin: install your digital wallet

You need Microsoft Authenticator, which is a secure digital wallet app. You will need to access the wallet to view all of your credentials.

What can you do with a digital wallet?

Use a digital wallet on your mobile or tablet devices to receive, store and share your passport credentials.

Read more about digital wallets and how to use them

Once your NHS Digital Staff Passport is set up, you can easily receive and share credentials each time you move to a new organisation.

To use Microsoft Authenticator

To use Microsoft Authenticator:

We strongly recommend backing up your digital wallet to the cloud. By doing so, you'll be able to recover your credentials if your phone is lost or stolen.

Download the free Microsoft Authenticator app from:

the App Store for iOS
the Google Play Store for Android

Follow the onscreen instructions to complete your setup.
