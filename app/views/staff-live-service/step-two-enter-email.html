Which email should I use?

Your email address is required at registration stage for verification. It will be used to provide formal communication about your activity and to advise of events within the portal that require you to take action, for example, to collect or share credentials.

Your email address must be accessible via your mobile device which also contains the digital wallet.

You will be using the Digital Staff Passport when you move permanently or temporarily between NHS organisations, so please choose an email account you access regularly and don't intend to change.
