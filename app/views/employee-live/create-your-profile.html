Create your Profile with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals

Complete the steps below to start creating your staff passport.

Verify your identity

Verify your identity either via a certified digital identity provider, or by booking an appointment with the HR team. To book an appointment, you will need to contact the HR team separately.

Read more about digital wallets and how to use them

Create your profile

Your passport profile contains some initial information about you. We'll need a passport-style digital photo of you (not needed if you share your digital identity), your email address and your mobile number.

Add information about your role such as your ESR assignment number or Oriel PIN, if relevant.

You'll receive an access key which you will need to add to your passport. Use this to login to this organisation and collect your NHS digital identity credential.

Your registration will then be complete. You'll be able to use your Digital Staff Passport to receive and share credentials with Sheffield Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust.
