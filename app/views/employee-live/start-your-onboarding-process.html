Start your onboarding process

Welcome. To begin your onboarding process with Sheffield Teaching Hospitals, please complete the steps below.

Share your credentials

Select and share the credentials fom your wallet, including your identity credential.

Create your passport profile

Add information about you and your role such as your Oriel PIN, if relevant. You'll be asked to verify your email address and your mobile number

You'll receive an access key which you will need to add to your passport. Use this to login to this organisation and collect your NHS digital identity credential.

Your onboarding process will then be complete. You'll be able to use your Digital Staff Passport to receive and share credentials with Sheffield Teaching Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust.
