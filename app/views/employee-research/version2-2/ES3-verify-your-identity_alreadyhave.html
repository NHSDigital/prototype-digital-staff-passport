Verify your identity with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals

You can set up and keep your digital identity on your smartphone with a government-approved app like Yoti or Post Office Easy ID. A digital identity is an online representation of your identity information, like your name or age.

You can use your verified digital identity to prove who you are when signing up for Digital Staff Passport.

A digital identity check can replace an in-person identity check with a member of your organisation's HR team.

You can still choose the in-person identity check if you can't verify your digital identity.
