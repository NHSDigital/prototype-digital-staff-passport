Create a verified digital identity

Download Yoti or Post Office EasyID

Get Yoti or Post Office EasyID from your smartphone app provider. Both apps are free to download and use.

You'll need to use your smartphone camera to scan your face and your passport on Yoti or Post Office EasyID.

Share your digital identity with your organisation

Select 'continue' once you have created your digital identity on Yoti or Post Office EasyID.

You'll be asked to share your identity by scanning a QR code in the next step.


What to do if you cannot create a digital identity

You can still sign up for DSP even if:

  • the digital identity check does not work for your passport or biometric card
  • you change your mind about creating a digital identity

Your identity can be verified through an in-person identity check with a member of the organisation's HR team instead.
