Start your onboarding

You are signing up for a Digital Staff Passport with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals. You can change the organisation if this isn't the right one.

Verify your identity with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals

You verify your identity by:

  • sharing your existing digital identity credential which is in your Microsoft Authenticator app, or
  • attending a face-to-face identity check with the HR team at your new organisation

Check your profile

Your existing Digital Staff Passport already stores your personal details, pre-employment checks and other important information.

Log into your profile to check and share essential employment information with your new organisation.

Once your identity is confirmed by your new trust, you'll receive an access key from the HR team. An access key is a virtual card that allows you to login to your new organisation and collect your digital identity credential. Add this access key to your Microsoft Authenticator app.

Your registration with your new organisation will then be complete. You'll be able to use your Digital Staff Passport to receive and share credentials with Doncaster and Bassetlaw Teaching Hospitals.
